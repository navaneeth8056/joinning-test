Speed Dating · 4-Person Rotation

Everyone meets everyone — on one shared clock

Three rounds. R1: (1·2) + (3·4). R2: (1·3) + (2·4). R3: (1·4) + (2·3). Everyone gathers in a lobby first; the clock starts once all four are in, and the same clock drives every round and break on all four screens. Between rounds the room ends and you get a countdown to your next partner; after the last round, a thank-you.
Who are you on this device?
Open this same page on all four devices. In the lobby, everyone clicks Log in with Google once — after that you are a moderator in every room you enter, so no pairing gets stuck waiting. The clock will not start until all four are in the lobby.
